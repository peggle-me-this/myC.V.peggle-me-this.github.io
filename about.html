A bit about me,

A self-proclaimed food geek, gamer, and nerd, I find joy in all creations in the kitchen and immersing myself in gaming worlds. I have found a new love for programming and I am investigating all I can about the vast domain of coding and technology.


With a passion for education, I've spent years shaping young minds and fostering a love for learning. Drawn to the ever-changing world of technology, I decided to explore coding, seeing it as a way to expand my creative horizons. The shift from the classroom to programming has been an exciting journey, fueled by a desire to understand and actively contribute to the digital realm. I have found joy in the blend of teaching experience and coding skills, not just mastering a new language but developing digital solutions and exploring the endless possibilities of coding. In bridging education and technology, my problem-solving skills acquired through teaching integrate effectively with coding, unlocking creativity, and new diverse opportunities.


Some of my other interests, which I feel strongly about, include personal growth, hiking, and gardening. Dedicating time to my growth and learning is very important to me. Exploring nature and exercising through hiking and cultivating beautiful spaces and food through gardening. I believe a holistic perspective is the overall nourishment of human life. Join me on this journey of exploration and growth, where each day brings new opportunities to learn, create, and inspire.
